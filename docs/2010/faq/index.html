Frequently Asked Questions

I've found a bug. What should I do?

We work hard to make sure that our hardware and software is bug-free, but some still manage to make their way through the cracks in our procedure. Discuss the problem you're having with the kit on our forums. We should be able to fix the bug and ship an update through RoboIDE within two or three days. Sometimes this will take longer. The closer to the competition you find the bug, the less likely we are to ship a fix, so make sure you tell us about bugs early.
